What happens after flashing

1

Touch calibration

The screen asks if the image is upside-down. Tap to flip if needed, then follow the corner arrows to calibrate touch. Done once, stored in flash.

2

Connect & configure

Join the ISS-Tracker-Setup WiFi on your phone or PC. Your browser opens automatically at 192.168.4.1. Fill in your WiFi, location and preferences.

3

Live tracking

The device reboots, connects to your WiFi and starts tracking immediately. No PC needed from this point on.

↺

Factory reset

Need to reconfigure? Hold the screen during the splash for 3 seconds, then confirm. All settings and calibration are wiped.

Features

📡

On-device SGP4 calculations

Full orbital math runs on the ESP32. No position API, no cloud dependency.

🗺️

World map & polar plot

Live footprint overlay, multi-pass prediction, azimuth/elevation graph.

🛰️

Any satellite

Track ISS, NOAA weather birds, HAM radio satellites, Hubble and more.

🔔

Audio notifications

Configurable buzzer beeps before AOS, LOS and at closest approach (TCA).

📶

WebSocket rotor output

Streams azimuth/elevation data for driving an external antenna rotor.

🔓

No API key needed

Timezone & DST retrieved automatically via Open-Meteo. Free, no registration.
